The model sees public stats only: Elo, age, layoffs, striking and grappling rates, accumulated damage, recent knockouts. It cannot see injuries, camp changes, weight cuts, or tape. This is a statistical model, not a tip sheet. Its verified edge averages +7% per bet with a confidence interval that nearly touches zero, and it went flat for a full year inside the backtest.
